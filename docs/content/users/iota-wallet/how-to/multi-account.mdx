# Manage Profiles and Accounts

IOTA Wallet allows you to use multiple profiles and accounts, offering flexibility in managing various assets or purposes for transactions.

:::tip Profiles vs. Accounts

A profile is a collection of accounts.

:::

## Add Another Account Address

You can create and use multiple accounts within the IOTA Wallet in two ways.

### Add a Profile

1. Go to `Accounts` by clicking on the address at the top of the `Home` section.

   ![Accounts](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/basics/home-tab.png)

2. Click `Add Profile`.

   ![Add Profile](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/manage-accounts.png)

3. Choose to either create a new wallet or import an existing wallet.

   ![Select Profile Type](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/add-profile.png)

### Add Another Mnemonic Account

1. Navigate to `Accounts` at the top of the `Home` tab and select `Manage`.

   ![Accounts](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/basics/home-tab.png)

2. Click the `+` icon next to the account type you want to add.

   ![Click plus icon](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/manage-accounts.png)

3. Your new account will appear. To use it, select it from the list in the `Manage Accounts` view, or choose the address when connecting your wallet to a site or app.

## Export a Public or Private Key for an Account

<Tabs queryString="key-type">
<TabItem value="public-key" label="Public Key">

Exporting a public key allows you to share or verify an account address without exposing sensitive information. Public keys are safe to share and are commonly used for identification, verification, or integration with external tools and services.

:::info

A public key with flag is a Base64-encoded representation of the account's public key that includes an additional prefix (flag) identifying the cryptographic scheme used (for example, Ed25519).

This format ensures the public key can be correctly interpreted by the IOTA protocol, SDKs, and tooling, and avoids ambiguity when working with different key types.

:::

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="private-key" label="Private Key">

Exporting a private key allows you to use the account with other wallet providers. Handle private keys with extreme caution to prevent unauthorized access to your assets.

:::danger

When viewing the private key, ensure no one can see your screen. Click the crossed-out eye icon in the bottom-right corner to reveal the key.

:::

</TabItem>
</Tabs>

1. Go to `Accounts` at the top of the `Home` tab.

   ![Accounts](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/basics/home-tab.png)

2. Select the account you want to export, click on the `...` icon, and choose `Export Account Keys` from the dropdown.

   ![Export Account Keys](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/multi-account/export-account-keys.png)

3. Enter your wallet password and click `Verify`.

   ![Verify](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/verify-password.png)

4. Click `Copy` to copy the private or public key with flag to your clipboard.

   ![Copy Public Key](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/multi-account/public-key-copy.png)


## Export a Mnemonic for an Account

Exporting a mnemonic allows you to use the account with other wallet providers. Handle your mnemonic with extreme caution to prevent unauthorized access to your assets.

:::danger

When viewing the mnemonic, ensure no one can see your screen. Click the crossed-out eye icon in the bottom-right corner to reveal the key.

:::

1. Go to `Accounts` at the top of the `Home` tab.

   ![Accounts](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/basics/home-tab.png)

2. Select the account you want to export, click on the `...` icon, and choose `Export Mnemonic` from the dropdown.

   ![Export Mnemonic](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/multi-account/export-mnemonic.png)

3. Enter your wallet password and click `Verify`.

   ![Verify](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/verify-password.png)

4. Click `Copy` to copy the mnemonic to your clipboard.

   ![Copy](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/multi-account/click-copy-mnemonic.png)
