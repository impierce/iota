To use your IOTA Wallet on different devices or browsers, you can import it using your 24-word mnemonic recovery phrase:

1. Open IOTA Wallet and select `Get Started`

![Add Profile](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/getting-started.png)

2. Choose `Add existing wallet`.

![Add Profile](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/add-profile.png)

3. Click on `Mnemonic`.

![Import Mnemonic](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/import-a-wallet.png)

4. Enter your 24-word mnemonic recovery phrase and click `Add Profile`.

![Add Profile](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/mnemonic.png)

5. Enter your desired password.

![Create Password](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/create-password.png)

6. Carefully read and accept the Terms of Service, and click `Create Wallet`.

![Create Wallet](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/create-wallet.png)

By following these steps, you can manage, stake,
and transfer your digital assets on the IOTA network from any of your devices, ensuring a seamless experience.
