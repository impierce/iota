# Importing Wallet using Passkey

This guide walks you through using IOTA Wallet with an existing Passkey account. By importing your Passkey, you can manage your assets directly within the IOTA Wallet.

1. Open IOTA Wallet and select `Get Started`

![Add Profile](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/getting-started.png)

2. Choose `Add existing wallet`.

![Add Profile](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/add-profile.png)

3. Click on `Passkey`.

![Passkey](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/import-a-wallet.png)

4. Click on `Continue`. Your device will guide you through the process of adding the Passkey account to the IOTA Wallet.

![Import Passkey](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/passkey/import-passkey.png)

5. Choose a nickname for your profile and click `Continue`.

![Profile Nickname](/img/users/iota-wallet/how-to/passkey/nickname.png)

6. Enter your desired password and click on `Create Wallet`.

![Create Password](/img/users/iota-wallet/getting-started/create-password.png)
