# Restore From Seed or Stronghold

If you have lost your mnemonic, you can still recover your account provided you have access to your seed or
`.stronghold` file.

<Seed />

## Back Up a Seed From a Firefly Profile

You can export you seed by clicking on the `...` menu, and then clicking `Backup Seed`.

![Backup Seed](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/backup-seed-falafel.png)

Firefly will then prompt you to enter your password.

![Enter Password](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/enter-password.png)

<Wait />

![Copy Seed](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/copy-seed.png)

With your seed at hand, you can [import your wallet using the IOTA Web Wallet](#import-accounts-from-a-seed).

## Back Up a Seed From a `.stronghold` file

:::info Not Recommended

If you are using Firefly, we recommend using the [Backup Seed](#back-up-a-seed-from-a-firefly-profile) option from the Firefly menu.
Using a stronghold file directly should only be done if you don't have a profile in Firefly or if you are using a different wallet.

:::

You can use your `.stronghold` backup file to obtain your wallet's seed.

If don't know where your `.stronghold` file is, click on the `...` menu and select the `Copy system profile location`. 

![Copy Stronghold Location](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/copy-stronghold-location.png)

<Tabs groupId="os" queryString>
   <TabItem value="windows" label="Windows">
      Next, you should click on the burger menu in the top left and `Firefly > Backup Seed`.

      ![Backup Seed Menu](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/backup-seed-menu-windows.png)
   </TabItem>
   <TabItem value="linux" label="Linux">
      Next, you should click on `Electron > Backup Seed`.

      ![Backup Seed Menu](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/backup-seed-menu-linux.png)
   </TabItem>
   <TabItem value="macos" label="MacOS">
      Next, in the menu bar click on `Firefly > Backup Seed`.

      ![Backup Seed Menu](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/backup-seed-menu-macos.png)
   </TabItem>
</Tabs>

Once you have the `.stronghold` file at hand, you can drag and drop it into Firefly's Stronghold Backup.

![Drag and Drop Stronghold](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/drag-and-drop-stronghold.png)

Firefly will then prompt you to enter your password to unlock the `.stronghold` file.

![Enter Password](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/enter-password.png)

<Wait />

Firefly will then show you the seed it restored from the `.stronghold` file. 

![Copy Seed](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/how-to/restore-account/copy-seed.png)

With your seed at hand, you can [import your wallet using the IOTA Web Wallet](#import-accounts-from-a-seed).

## Import Accounts from a Seed

<Seed />

To use your IOTA Wallet on different devices or browsers, you can import it using your Seed:

1. Open IOTA Wallet and select `Add Profile`

![Add Profile](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/getting-started/add-profile.png)

2. Click the `Mnemonic` button in the `Import` section.

![Import Mnemonic](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/getting-started/click-import-mnemonic.png)

3. Enter your Seed and click `Add Profile`.

![Add Profile](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/getting-started/mnemonic.png)

4. Enter your desired password.

![Create Password](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/getting-started/create-password.png)

5. Carefully read and accept the Terms of Service, and click `Create Wallet`.

![Create Wallet](/img/about-iota/iota-wallet/getting-started/create-wallet.png)

By following these steps, you can manage, stake,
and transfer your digital assets on the IOTA network from any of your devices, ensuring a seamless experience.
